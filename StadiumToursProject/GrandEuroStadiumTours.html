ABOUT

Grand European Stadium Tours, or GEST, is an immersive football experience unlike any other. Dive into the history, culture, and tradition of the most famous football clubs in Europe. From Barcelona's Camp Nou to Everton's Goodison Park, nothing compares to the atmosphere inside these cathedrals of the beautiful game.

STADIUMS

Experience the thrill of stepping inside the most iconic football grounds across Europe, including:

  • Amsterdam Arena, home of AFC Ajax
  • Parc des Princes, home of Paris Saint-Germain
  • Wanda Metropolitana, home of Atletico Madrid
  • Santiago Bernabeau, home of Real Madrid CF
  • Camp Nou, home of FC Barcelona
  • Wembley Stadium, home of Tottenham Hotspur FC
  • Goodison Park, home of Everton FC
  • Emirates Stadium, home of Arsenal FC
